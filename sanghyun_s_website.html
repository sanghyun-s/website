About
Where accounting
expertise meets AI.

I started my career in tax accounting at Rowshan & Co, spending hundreds of hours manually processing 1099s and W-2s every tax season. That repetitive, error-prone process planted a question: what if AI could handle this?

Pursuing my MS in Business Analytics, I dove into Python, machine learning, and generative AI — not as abstract concepts, but as tools to solve the real problems I'd lived through. The result: three production-ready applications that automate tax document processing, enable natural language accounting queries, and detect audit anomalies.

My differentiator isn't just knowing Python or AI. It's understanding why a TIN mismatch on a 1099-NEC matters, what auditors actually look for in GL transactions, and how QuickBooks data maps to GAAP categories. Domain expertise + AI engineering — that's my edge.
